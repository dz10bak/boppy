Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use Boppy, we may collect the following information:

  • Prompts and song descriptions you submit
  • Generated audio metadata (duration, genre, timestamps)

No personal data (name, email, address) is required to use Boppy.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Generate music based on your prompts
  • Deliver content back to you
  • Operate and improve the service

3. Third-Party Services

Your prompts are sent to large language models for lyrics and caption composition.

Boppy is hosted on a VPS. Your data is stored on this server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@boppy.me.

6. Cookies

Boppy uses minimal cookies for session management only. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@boppy.me.
